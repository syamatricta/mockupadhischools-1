Real Estate Broker

A real estate broker has to meet several qualifications before they can be licensed. First, you must be 18 years old or older. You must also be a United States citizen. You do not have to be a resident of the state where you get your license, but you may have to meet additional requirements according to the state that is issuing the license. A real estate broker will also have to submit a set of finger prints. They will undergo a back ground check, as well. If there is any criminal past they may not be eligible to be a real estate agent or a broker. You must serve under a real estate broker as a licensed real estate agent for at least two years before becoming a broker yourself. The real estate sales experience has to be within the past five years or you may have to meet additional qualifications.

A real estate broker also has to meet additional educational requirements before taking the exam for the real estate brokers license. You need to find a good school that can help you train and prepare for your real estate broker exam. If you are prepared for the exam you will be far more likely to pass it on your first try. Some schools offer this training in a class room setting and other schools offer online classes that can easily fit into your busy schedule.A real estate broker course will help you to prepare, but much of the study has to be done on your own. You can make really good money as a real estate agent, and even more money as a real estate broker. You can then have other real estate agents working under you just as you worked under your broker. It is a great way to move up in the professional world.A real estate broker makes good money when compared to other professions with similar educational requirements.

You do not have to spend years in school in order to get a degree to become a real estate agent or broker. The school is also less expensive than many two year degrees or four year degrees from public or private colleges.A real estate broker also has to maintain a code of ethics. The licensing body, such as the Bureau of Real Estate, will over see ethical issues regarding the people that they license. After you apply for your exam, take the exam and pass the exam, you will need to apply to the licensing body to get your license.A real estate broker needs to keep their license in good standing. This includes paying any dues that are owed, keeping up your training, education and current professional resume as well as maintaining the code of ethics established by the licensing body.A real estate broker course and classes can be found online. Choosing a good school is an important first step in getting your license. The right school will be able to prepare you for the exam as well as your career once you get your license.
