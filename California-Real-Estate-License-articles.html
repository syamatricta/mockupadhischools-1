California Real Estate License

A California real estate license can give you the power to put yourself and your family into a better financial situation. If the economy or a lack of education has held you back, a real estate license is a good way to earn a good living and to help other people get the homes of their dreams at the same time. The world of real estate is challenging, but it is also very rewarding. A California real estate license starts with at least three college level classes. Special programs are available that will fully train you to be a good real estate agent in the state of California. The great thing about a real estate license is that you can get a license to sell real estate in any state that you choose. You can even get a California real estate license if you do not live in the state of California. A California real estate license can only be granted to United States citizens age 18 or older. You must first complete at least three classes before you apply to take your real estate exam.

You must take the class of Real Estate Principles, the class of Real Estate Practice and one class from a list of approved classes.A California real estate license exam must be applied for, taken and passed. Then you can apply for the actual license. If one is granted, you will become a licensed real estate agent and you can sell real estate in the state of California under a licensed real estate broker. If you choose one day to become a broker yourself, you will need to meet additional qualifications and pass another exam. A California real estate license requires you to submit fingerprints and to pass a background check. Certain crimes, no matter how long ago they occurred, may prevent you from getting a license to sell real estate. It is important that you be completely honest about your past when you are applying to take your exam and when you apply for your actual real estate license. A California real estate license is granted by the Bureau of Real Estate, or CalBRE.

The CalBRE publishes a Reference Book - A Real Estate Guide for people who would like to learn more about becoming a real estate agent. You can get a copy of this manual online or from any CalBRE office. If you get one at the office, there will be a charge.A California real estate license is best gotten after taking a course. Correspondence or online courses are a great way to get your real estate education. These programs are designed for busy professionals. Classroom settings are another good way to learn what you need to know to pass your real estate exam and get your license.A California real estate license is a good thing to have this day and time. There will always be a need for more houses, and there will always be a need for qualified people to sell those houses.
