California Real Estate Exam

The California real estate exam is the final step in becoming a licensed real estate agent, but it is far from the only step. Before you take the exam, you will need to have several classes to prepare you for it and to meet the college level requirements of having the license. You will not be allowed to take the exam until these requirements are met. Fortunately, there are schools that can help you meet these requirements and prepare you to take the examThe California real estate exam can be taken if you are at least 18 years old and are a United States citizen as soon as the required classes are taken and passed. You must apply to take the exam, take the exam and pass it before you can apply for your real estate license. The Department of Real Estate is the licensing body for all real estate agents.The California real estate exam will qualify you to be a real estate agent. You can take it and get your license even if you do not live in the state of California.

As long as you are a citizen of the United States 18 years old or older you can meet the requirements for a California real estate license. After you take the exam and pass you can apply for your license. If you are approved and receive your license you can act as a real estate agent in the state of California under a licensed real estate broker.There is a separate California real estate exam to take if you wish to become a real estate broker. First you must have worked for at least two years as a licensed real estate agent operating under another broker. Then you must pass additional college level classes. Most of the schools that will train you to be a real estate agent also offer the classes you need to become a real estate broker.Then you can take the California real estate exam to become a broker. You must apply to take the exam, pass the exam and then you can apply to the Department of Real Estate (DRE) to get your license.

It is a rewarding career. Considering the amount of education necessary to get your real estate agent or real estate broker license, you can make a good amount of money selling real estate.The California real estate exam is usually easier for students to pass when they have been to a school that specializes in training real estate agents. Some of these schools offer classes on campus and others offer all of the classes that you need to become a licensed agent online. If you are working a full time job it may be easier for you to attend class online rather than have to go to class every week.a real estate exam is given by the DRE to make sure that all of the agents selling real estate have the proper training. The licensing body over sees ethics as well as many legal matters. Taking the California real estate exam is a positive step toward your future.
